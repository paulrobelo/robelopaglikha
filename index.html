About this website

PA-G-LIKHA is a website portfolio that shows works and creations of Paul Angelo P. Robelo.
PA-G-LIKHA stands for PA(Paul Angelo) -G (Gelo) - Likha (Creation) PAGLIKHA defines the act of creating and derived from the creativity of individuals with the ability to produce a variety of objects, producing works. This Website shows the profile and collections of projects including Hardware and Software.

My mission is to connect the world through my works and experiences.
